Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, browse our collection, select your desired items, choose size and quantity, add to cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using various methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfer, or cash on delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods including:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • JazzCash & EasyPaisa
  • USDT (Cryptocurrency)

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location:

  • Lahore: 1-2 business days
  • Major cities (Karachi, Islamabad, Faisalabad): 2-3 business days
  • Other cities: 3-5 business days
  • International shipping: 7-14 business days

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items in original condition. Returns are accepted for:

  • Defective or damaged items
  • Wrong size or color
  • Items not matching description

Custom-made items and sale items are not eligible for returns.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can track your order by:

  • Visiting our Track Order page
  • Using the courier company's website
  • Calling our customer service at 0341 9748504

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship internationally to selected countries. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific rates and availability for your country.

How do I choose the right size?

We provide detailed size charts for each product. You can also:

  • Check our Size Guide page
  • Contact our customer service for assistance
  • Schedule a fitting appointment at our Lahore store

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be cancelled or modified within 2 hours of placement. After this time, we begin processing and changes may not be possible. Please contact us immediately at 0341 9748504 for any changes.

Do you offer custom tailoring?

Yes, we offer custom tailoring services for most of our products. Custom orders require:

  • Detailed measurements
  • 2-3 weeks processing time
  • 50% advance payment
  • Final fitting appointment
